Choose the optimization mode for previewing the outcomes of specific stroke types:

* **Fast:** Provides a quicker preview with a slight compromise on quality. Ideal for documents with numerous dense strokes.
* **Normal:** Standard rendering mode.
* **Best:** Delivers the highest quality rendering.

Note that this setting won't impact the quality of the final exported result.
