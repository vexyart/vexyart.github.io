Vexy Lines turns a photo, drawing, or generated image into expressive, razor-sharp vector art. Drop in a bitmap. Pick from over a dozen algorithmic fills: engraved lines, waves, halftones, spirals, scribbles, fractals, text of varying weight, or hand-made directional strokes that the algorithm fills in. Stack the fills on layers with masks and mesh for precise control. 

Strokes pull thickness (and optionally color) straight from your image source. Export crisp and infinitely scalable SVG or PDF ready for print, vinyl, laser, or screen.

## What Makes Vexy Lines Special?

Vexy Lines offers a unique approach to vector creation, focusing on transforming images using sophisticated line and shape fills. Key features include:

*   **Expressive Line Effects:** Create beautiful engraving and hatching effects with precise control over line density, direction, and style.
*   **Unique Textures:** Generate patterns using text, fractals, and organic wave structures.
*   **Intelligent Tracing:** Convert bitmap images into clean, scalable vector paths automatically.
*   **Customizable Style:** Develop your signature look with highly adjustable fills, strokes, and effects.
*   **Flexible Output:** Export production-ready vector files (SVG, PDF, EPS) and raster images (PNG, JPEG) for print, web, and other design applications.
